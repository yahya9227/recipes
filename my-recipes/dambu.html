Dambu

Description

A beautiful Dambu at home

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish (whole or fillets)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Seasonings
  • Flour or cornmeal (for coating)
  • Optional: eggs or buttermilk (for dredging)
  • Oil (vegetable, canola, or peanut oil)

Steps

  1. Clean the Fish
  2. Season the Fish
  3. Prepare Coating Station
  4. Coat the Fish
  5. Heat the Oil
  6. Fry the Fish
  7. Drain the Fish
  8. Serve
